rustychickpeas (this engine) vs Kùzu, same data, same seeds (pick_seeds).

Median of 5 each. IS5 is not timed on the rust side. rustychickpeas wins the
multi-hop traversals (IC2/IC9) and the sub-millisecond short reads (IS2/IS3);
Kùzu's native path engine is competitive on the single IC13 shortest path.

Feasible tier reuses the faithful Message/Person/knows/hasCreator/replyOf
projection (kuzu/db-sf1-faithful, read-only). Deferred on the Kùzu side:
IC1/IS1 (the faithful Person projection omits firstName/lastName) and IC14
(interaction-weight semantics).
